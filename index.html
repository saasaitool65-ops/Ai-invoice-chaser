FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

I completed payment but my plan wasn't upgraded — what should I do? ▼
If your payment was successful but the plan upgrade didn't reflect, please email support@invoicechaser.com with your payment ID and registered email address. We will manually activate your plan within 24 hours — your payment is completely safe.
Is my payment secure? ▼
Absolutely. We use Lemon Squeezy, a trusted international payment processor. Your payment is processed directly through a secure gateway. If you face any issue, a full refund is guaranteed within 7 days — no questions asked.
What is the 7-day refund policy? ▼
If you are not satisfied within 7 days of purchase, you will receive a full refund — no questions asked. Simply email support@invoicechaser.com with the subject line "Refund Request".
What does the Free plan include? ▼
The Free plan includes 3 active invoices, AI email generation, and a Day-3 reminder. No credit card required — simply sign up and start using it right away.
How do I cancel my plan? ▼
Go to Settings → Subscription → Cancel anytime. There is no lock-in period. After cancellation, you will retain access until the end of your current billing cycle.
Can I upgrade from Pro to Business? ▼
Yes! Go to Dashboard → Settings → Upgrade Plan. The remaining pro-rated amount is automatically adjusted — you only pay the difference.
